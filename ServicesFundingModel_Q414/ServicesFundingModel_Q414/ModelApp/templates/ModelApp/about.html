    Guiding Principles

    Adam Karon | SVP Global Services & Support

    The Services Capacity Model follows the core philosophy that every product sold requires some professional services, (either during initial installation or ongoing maintenance), and that every consultant has finite bandwidth based on target utilization rates. These two components represent the demand and supply side of the model, respectively, and are at the heart of its functionality. For more information on Model Principles please consult with your local SD-Ops representative.
